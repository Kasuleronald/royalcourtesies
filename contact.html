Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large events, and 1-2 months for corporate events. However, we can accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability.

Do you offer packages or custom solutions?

We provide both pre-designed packages for common event types and fully customized solutions tailored to your specific needs, preferences, and budget.

What areas in Uganda do you serve?

We primarily serve Kampala and surrounding areas, but we're available for events throughout Uganda. Additional travel fees may apply for locations outside Kampala.

Can you work with my budget?

Absolutely! We believe exceptional events can be created at various budget levels. We'll work closely with you to maximize your budget while delivering outstanding quality.
